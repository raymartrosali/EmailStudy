Learn More About Trending Franchise Ideas at 8th Philippine SME Business Conference


Learn the "Trending Franchise Ideas to Watch Out" from Mr. Rudolf Kotik, Founder of RK Franchise Consultancy, on May 03, 2019 from 1:55 pm - 2:40 pm at the Conference Area, SMX Convention Center, Mall of Asia Complex, Pasay City.

ABOUT THE SPEAKER

Rudolf Kotik, Founder of RK Franchise Consultancy Inc., originally from Vienna, Austria, has been in the Philippine Industry for more than 40 years in 3 continents. Operating the RK Franchise Consultancy since 1997, he has developed more than 800 franchise companies into franchise systems. His company expanded globally and currently developing companies from GCC, Australia and other ASEAN countries. He is also the co-founder of Filipino International Franchise Association Inc. The association assists their franchise queries and a contributor in the Franchising PH Magazine published by the association.
